#TEXT: ;;; A decided success.;;; --Mr. John Drew, so well known in theatrical circles all over the United States, made his first appearance on the night of the 23d October, in Dublin, Ireland, at the Theatre Royal, in the roles of Sir Patrick O'Plenipo, in the Irish Ambassador, and Handy Andy, in the play of that name.;;; The Freeman's Journal says that his acting was a decided success.;;;
