#TEXT: ;;; Inquest Room.;;; --An appropriation for an inquest room was made by the Council some time ago, but nothing more has been done about it. It is unnecessary to repeat the reasons why the building ought to be constructed, as they are obvious enough to the public.;;; We merely desire to call the attention of the authorities to the matter.;;; An instance occurred during the past week, wherein the necessity of a place to deposit unrecognized bodies was perfectly apparent.;;;
