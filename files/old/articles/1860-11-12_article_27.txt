#TEXT: ;;; Hungarian officers required to serve as privates.;;; --A year ago a number of Hungarian officers, said to be not less than eight hundred, who resigned their commissions in the Austrian army, have been informed that they are all comprised in the conscription now in progress, and must stand the chances of the ballot with the other young men of their age.;;;
