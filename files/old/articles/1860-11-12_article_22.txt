#TEXT: ;;; A Circus Baron.;;; --The London American announces that Messrs. Howes &amp; Cushing's American Circus, which has been on exhibition for some time in England, will be sold on the 17th inst.;;; Mr. Howe, who retires upon a large fortune, purposes buying a large estate upon the Rhine, upon which is a baronial castle.;;; The title accompanying the estate will give the American circus proprietor the title of Baron Howes.;;;
