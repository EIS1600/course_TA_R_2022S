#TEXT: ;;; Blown up.;;; --At Newburyport, Mass., while Jacob Morrill, John R. Kimball and Wm. P. Bartlett were firing a cannon in honor of "Old Abe's" election, on Wednesday, the gun blew up, and injured them all more or less.--They were all ferrymen, and Morrill was over fifty years of age.;;;
