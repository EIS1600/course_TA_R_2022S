#TEXT: ;;; The case of Sherman Booth.;;; --An application has been made to the President by Sherman Booth, the negro rescuer, who has given the Federal authorities of Wisconsin so much trouble, for remission of his fine, on the ground of poverty, but the application is accompanied with insolent defiance and determination on his part to repeat the offence upon the next opportunity.;;;
